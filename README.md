District and Tehsil Updates

Nadra Data has more recent districts so PBS Census Data has been updated to reflect following (and in some places NADRA data had some changes needed)

T.A. ADJ Tehsils – All T.A. ADJ tehsils (e.g., T.A. ADJ BANNU, T.A. ADJ D.I.KHAN, etc.) belong to the district mentioned in their name post FATA merger. like T.A. ADJ BANNU is part of Bannu district. 

Talagang – District created in 2022 from Talagang and Lawa Tehsils (previously part of Chakwal).

Murree – District created in 2022 from Murree and Kotli Sattian Tehsils (previously part of Rawalpindi).

Wazirabad – District created by removing Wazirabad Tehsil from Gujranwala.

Kot Addu – District created by removing Kot Addu and Chowk Sarwar Shaheed Tehsils from Muzaffargarh.

Chitral – NADRA data corrected to Lower Chitral.

Kohistan – NADRA uses “Kohistan,” census lists Upper Kohistan (correct name).

Hub – District created in 2022 from Hub, Dureji, Sonmiani, and Gadani Tehsils (from Lasbela).

Usta Muhammad – District created from Usta Muhammad and Gandakha Tehsils (from Jafarabad).
